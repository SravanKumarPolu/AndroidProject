1. Introduction

Welcome to ImpulseVault ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

ImpulseVault is a mobile application designed to help you avoid impulsive purchases by enforcing a cool-down period and tracking your spending decisions. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you use our mobile application.

By using ImpulseVault, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

When you use ImpulseVault, you may provide the following information:

  • Impulse Data: Information about purchases you log, including:
    • Item or purchase title
    • Category (e.g., Food, Electronics, Entertainment)
    • Price (optional)
    • Emotion tags (optional)
    • Urgency level
    • Notes or comments (optional)
    • Timestamps (creation date, review date, execution date)
  • App Settings: Your preferences and settings within the app, including:
    • Notification preferences
    • Cool-down period settings
    • Display preferences
    • First use date

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: We may collect certain information automatically, including:
    • Device type and model
    • Operating system version
    • App version
    • Unique device identifiers (for app functionality only)
  • Usage Data: We may collect information about how you interact with the app, such as:
    • Features used
    • Time spent in the app
    • Error logs (for app improvement)

2.3 Cloud Sync (Optional)

If you enable cloud sync in the app settings:

  • Your impulse data and settings are stored securely on Supabase servers
  • We use anonymous authentication (no email or password required)
  • Data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Each user's data is isolated using Row Level Security (RLS)
Note: Cloud sync is optional. You can use the app entirely offline with local storage only.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide Core Functionality: Enable the app's primary features, including:
    • Logging and tracking impulses
    • Enforcing cool-down periods
    • Calculating statistics (money saved, regret rates, streaks)
    • Sending notifications for review reminders
  • Improve the App: Analyze usage patterns to:
    • Fix bugs and errors
    • Enhance user experience
    • Develop new features
    • Optimize app performance
  • Cloud Sync: If enabled, synchronize your data across devices securely
  • Legal Compliance: Comply with applicable laws and regulations

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

  • Your data is stored locally on your device using secure storage mechanisms
  • Data remains on your device and is not transmitted unless you enable cloud sync
  • You can delete all local data at any time through the app settings

4.2 Cloud Storage (Optional)

  • If cloud sync is enabled, your data is stored on Supabase servers
  • Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest
  • We use Row Level Security (RLS) to ensure data isolation between users
  • Supabase complies with industry-standard security practices

4.3 Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption of data in transit
  • Secure authentication mechanisms
  • Regular security assessments
  • Access controls and data isolation

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.
